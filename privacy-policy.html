1. Kirish

MIGO — O'zbekistondagi mobil marketplace ilovasi bo'lib, foydalanuvchilarga xalqaro bozorlardan (asosan Taobao) mahsulotlar sotib olish imkonini beradi. Ushbu Maxfiylik siyosati MIGO ilovasi sizning shaxsiy ma'lumotlaringizni qanday yig'ishi, ishlatishi, saqlashi va himoya qilishini tushuntiradi.

Ilovani ishlatib siz ushbu siyosatga rozilik bildirgan hisoblanasiz. Agar ba'zi shartlarga rozi bo'lmasangiz, iltimos ilovadan foydalanmang.

2. Yig'iladigan ma'lumotlar

2.1 Siz to'g'ridan-to'g'ri taqdim etadigan ma'lumotlar

  • Hisob ma'lumotlari: ism, familiya, email manzil, telefon raqami, parol (xashlangan holatda).
  • Yetkazib berish manzili: shahar, ko'cha, uy raqami, qabul qiluvchi ismi va telefoni.
  • Buyurtma ma'lumotlari: tanlangan mahsulotlar, miqdori, narxi, buyurtma sanasi.
  • Aloqa xabarlari: qo'llab-quvvatlash xizmatiga yo'llaган xabarlaringiz.

2.2 Avtomatik yig'iladigan ma'lumotlar

  • Qurilma ma'lumotlari: qurilma turi, operatsion tizim versiyasi, til sozlamalari.
  • Foydalanish statistikasi: ko'rilgan mahsulotlar, qidiruv tarixi, sevimlilar ro'yxati — ilova tavsiyalarini yaxshilash uchun.
  • Texnik loglar: xatoliklar haqida xabarlar (crash reports), ishlash ko'rsatkichlari — anonim.

2.3 Rasm orqali qidiruv

Rasm bilan qidiruv funksiyasini ishlatganda yuklagan rasmingiz 2 daqiqa davomida serverda saqlanadi, qidiruv natijasi qaytarilgach avtomatik o'chiriladi. Rasmlar boshqa maqsadda ishlatilmaydi.

3. To'lov ma'lumotlari

Karta raqamingiz BIZ TOMONDAN SAQLANMAYDI. Barcha to'lovlar Click to'lov tizimi orqali amalga oshiriladi. Karta raqami va amal muddati to'g'ridan-to'g'ri Click serveriga uzatiladi, biz faqat token oramiz — bu token bilan kelajakda to'lov qila olmaysiz (har gal yangi karta kiritiladi).
  • To'lov ma'lumotlari Click Merchant API orqali xavfsiz tarzda uzatiladi (HTTPS + sha1 imzo).
  • SMS tasdiqlash kodi Click tomonidan yuboriladi — biz uni ko'rmaymiz.
  • Click maxfiylik siyosatiга rioya qiladi: click.uz/privacy

4. Ma'lumotlardan qanday foydalanamiz

  • Buyurtmalarni qabul qilish va yetkazib berish.
  • To'lovlarni qayta ishlash.
  • Foydalanuvchi hisobini boshqarish va xavfsiz kirish.
  • Mahsulot tavsiyalarini shaxsiylashtirish (algoritm asosida).
  • Ilova ishini yaxshilash va xatoliklarni tuzatish.
  • Qonun talab qilgan hollarда rasmiy organlarga ma'lumot taqdim etish.

Sizning ma'lumotlaringiz marketing maqsadiда uchinchi tomonga sotilmaydi.

5. Uchinchi tomon xizmatlari

MIGO quyidagi tashqi xizmatlardan foydalanadi:

  • Click (click.uz) — to'lovlarni qayta ishlash. Karta ma'lumoti Click'ga uzatiladi.
  • Supabase (supabase.com) — ma'lumotlarni saqlash va autentifikatsiya. Ma'lumotlar shifrlangan tarzda saqlanadi.
  • Taobao / RapidAPI — mahsulot kataloglariga ulanish. Sizning shaxsiy ma'lumotlaringiz Taobao'ga yuborilmaydi.
  • Expo / Google — push bildirishnomalar va ilova yangilanishi.
  • Google Translate API — mahsulot nomlarini tarjima qilish (faqat mahsulot matni, shaxsiy emas).

6. Ma'lumot xavfsizligi

  • Barcha so'rovlar HTTPS/TLS orqali shifrlanadi.
  • Parol bcrypt yoki shunga o'xshash xeshlash algoritmi bilan saqlanadi.
  • Ma'lumotlar bazasiда Row Level Security (RLS) yoqilgan — har foydalanuvchi faqat o'z ma'lumotini ko'radi.
  • Maxfiy kalitlar (API tokens) Supabase Vault'да shifrlangan saqlanadi.

7. Sizning huquqlaringiz

Siz quyidagi huquqlarga egasiz:

  • Ko'rish huquqi: bizда saqlangan o'z ma'lumotingizni so'rab olish.
  • Tahrirlash huquqi: noto'g'ri yoki eskirgan ma'lumotni tuzatish (ilova ichidan profilda yoki yuqoridagi email orqali).
  • O'chirish huquqi: hisobingizni va u bilan bog'liq barcha ma'lumotlarni butunlay o'chirish.
  • Roziligini qaytarib olish: har qachon ilovadан chiqib, hisobingizни o'chirib tashlash.

So'rovingizni quyidagi aloqaga yuboring — 7 ish kuni ichida javob beramiz.

  • Anonim statistika cheksiz vaqt saqlanishi mumkin (shaxsni aniqlay olmaydigan).

9. Bolalar maxfiyligi

MIGO 16 yoshdan kichik bolalar uchun mo'ljallanmagan. Biz 16 yoshgacha bo'lgan foydalanuvchilarning shaxsiy ma'lumotlarini bila turib yig'maymiz. Agar ota-ona/vasiy o'z farzandi ma'lumotini biz to'plaganini aniqlasa, iltimos aloqa qiling — darhol o'chiramiz.

10. Siyosat o'zgarishi

Ushbu siyosat vaqti-vaqti bilan yangilanishi mumkin. Muhim o'zgarishlar haqida foydalanuvchilarga email yoki ilova ichida bildirishnoma yuboriladi. Yuqoridagi "Oxirgi yangilangan" sanasi o'zgarish kunini ko'rsatadi.

1. Introduction

MIGO is a mobile marketplace application based in Uzbekistan that allows users to purchase products from international markets (primarily Taobao). This Privacy Policy explains how MIGO collects, uses, stores and protects your personal information.

By using the app, you agree to this policy. If you do not agree with any part of it, please do not use the app.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide Directly

  • Account information: first name, last name, email address, phone number, password (hashed).
  • Delivery address: city, street, house number, recipient name and phone.
  • Order information: selected products, quantity, price, order date.
  • Communication messages: messages you send to customer support.

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Device information: device type, operating system version, language settings.
  • Usage statistics: viewed products, search history, favorites — to improve recommendations.
  • Technical logs: crash reports, performance metrics — anonymous.

2.3 Image Search

When you use image-based search, your uploaded image is stored on our server for 2 minutes and is automatically deleted after the search result is returned. Images are not used for any other purpose.

3. Payment Information

YOUR CARD NUMBER IS NOT STORED BY US. All payments are processed through the Click payment system. Card number and expiration date are transmitted directly to Click's server, and we only receive a token — this token cannot be reused for future payments (you must enter the card each time).
  • Payment information is securely transmitted via the Click Merchant API (HTTPS + sha1 signature).
  • SMS verification codes are sent by Click — we do not see them.
  • Click follows its own privacy policy: click.uz/privacy

4. How We Use Your Information

  • To accept and deliver orders.
  • To process payments.
  • To manage user accounts and secure login.
  • To personalize product recommendations (algorithmic).
  • To improve app performance and fix issues.
  • To provide information to authorities when legally required.

We do not sell your data to third parties for marketing.

5. Third-Party Services

MIGO uses the following external services:

  • Click (click.uz) — payment processing. Card data is transmitted to Click.
  • Supabase (supabase.com) — data storage and authentication. Data is stored encrypted.
  • Taobao / RapidAPI — product catalog access. Your personal data is not sent to Taobao.
  • Expo / Google — push notifications and app updates.
  • Google Translate API — translating product names (product text only, not personal data).

6. Data Security

  • All requests are encrypted via HTTPS/TLS.
  • Passwords are stored using bcrypt or a similar hashing algorithm.
  • Row Level Security (RLS) is enabled on the database — each user sees only their own data.
  • Secret keys (API tokens) are stored encrypted in Supabase Vault.

7. Your Rights

You have the following rights:

  • Right to access: request a copy of the data we hold about you.
  • Right to correct: fix inaccurate or outdated information (in-app via profile or via the contact below).
  • Right to delete: permanently remove your account and all associated data.
  • Right to withdraw consent: log out and delete your account at any time.

Send requests to the contact below — we respond within 7 business days.

8. Data Retention

  • While the account is active — until you delete it.
  • After account deletion — 30 days (for legal/financial reporting requirements), then permanently removed.
  • Anonymous statistics may be retained indefinitely (cannot identify individuals).

9. Children's Privacy

MIGO is not intended for children under 16. We do not knowingly collect personal data from users under 16. If a parent/guardian discovers that we have collected such data, please contact us — we will delete it immediately.

10. Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated from time to time. Significant changes will be communicated via email or in-app notification. The "Last updated" date above reflects the most recent change.

MIGO — мобильное маркетплейс-приложение в Узбекистане, позволяющее покупать товары с международных рынков (в основном Taobao). Настоящая Политика конфиденциальности описывает, как MIGO собирает, использует, хранит и защищает ваши персональные данные.

2. Какие данные мы собираем

2.1 Данные, которые вы предоставляете напрямую

  • Данные учётной записи: имя, фамилия, email, номер телефона, пароль (в виде хеша).
  • Адрес доставки: город, улица, дом, имя и телефон получателя.
  • Данные заказа: выбранные товары, количество, цена, дата заказа.

2.2 Данные, собираемые автоматически

  • Данные устройства: тип устройства, версия ОС, языковые настройки.
  • Статистика использования: просмотренные товары, история поиска, избранное — для улучшения рекомендаций.
  • Технические логи: отчёты о сбоях, метрики производительности — анонимно.

2.3 Поиск по изображению

При использовании поиска по изображению загруженный вами снимок хранится на сервере 2 минуты и автоматически удаляется после возврата результатов поиска. Изображения не используются ни для каких других целей.

НОМЕР ВАШЕЙ КАРТЫ НЕ СОХРАНЯЕТСЯ У НАС. Все платежи осуществляются через систему Click. Номер карты и срок действия передаются напрямую на сервер Click, а мы получаем только токен — этот токен нельзя использовать повторно для будущих платежей (карту нужно вводить заново при каждой покупке).

  • Приём и доставка заказов.
  • Обработка платежей.

  • Персонализация рекомендаций товаров (алгоритмически).

MIGO использует следующие внешние сервисы:
